KӨK QALPAK QOY / BLUE HAT GOAT

Discovered by / at:

Tansholpan Zhanabekova, 2022

Family:

The creature that the Kazakh people first discovered seemed like a normal sheep on the surface. However, when you interacted with it, it revealed some amazing abilities. Its family belonged to a SpaceX corporation, so it could transform into a space-traveling animal. After the SpaceX Corporation's destruction of the world, this sheep learned the skill of jumping between spaces and worlds. And this is how he discovered Kazakhstan at the first place.

Habitat:

In betwwen of space and Kazakhstan, particular Astana

Appearance:

He looks like a normal sheep with the traditional Kazakh hat, the Qalpak, and a tail which was cut off due to the destruction of the world as he was trying to save his family. We see that he moves every time. This is his superpower, that he can fly and move anywhere within the location you are in right now. However, as soon as you try to make him stop, he rolls his eyes.

Key Characteristics:

  • Likes to move around
  • Has some Kazakh roots
  • Travels in Times(Dimensions)
  • Changhes his size

What we know so far about KӨK QALPAK QOY? This KӨK QALPAK QOY is a very rare creature that saved from the world’s destruction. So he is actually very lonely, but doesn’t show it andalways smiles. Especially as he sees knew guests in the space, he wants to show them something that reminds him of the home. And its a Kazakh’s steppe with the beautiful nature, mountains, and a traditional architecture. As there were some natural changes during the apocalypse, his body had to learn how to transform in order to survive.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Because he needed to survive during an apocalyptic situation, he had to learn how to change the size of his body in order to fit into cracks and hide from the bright light of the stars. Think about the key that is designed to fit the body's size and shape.
